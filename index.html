About Me


I am a teacher with a fondness for continual development.I have a background in psychology having completed an undergraduate degree at the University of Birmingham. Over the years, I have gradually gained an insight into the importance of technology and its vital role in developing young minds and granting pupils of all background access. Seeing this impact for myself, in own classroom, has inspired me to delve further into the world of tech and train to become a full stack web developer.
